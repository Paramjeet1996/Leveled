The problem

Small businesses are the cornerstone of the American economy providing ~50% of all employment. A lot of these businesses are owned by boomers, who will be retiring in the next decade.

70% of these retiring owners do not have a succession plan. This will result in $10T assets available for sale. This is going to set the stage for the largest wealth transfer of our generation from baby boomers to millennials.

However, most millennials are in corporate jobs and cannot work out the time / money / risk tradeoffs to buy and operate these businesses. They will either shut down or be taken over by Private Equity / large corporations resulting in job losses and wealth concentration.

Our solution

Leveler is an innovative platform that is attempting to democratize small business ownership. Our aim is to sustainably run these businesses and continue to provide employment to the community and fuel the local economy.

At Leveler, we buy small businesses from retiring owners, make operational improvements and put capable operators in charge. We provide a path for operators to own these businesses with as little as $10,000 and in turn earn a share of the cash flow generated in the form of dividends. All this without any time investment from our customers!

Over time, we will look for opportunities to further grow and scale these businesses to maximize value for our investors.
